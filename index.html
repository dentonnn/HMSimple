16-Week Training Plan

10K Goal: Sub-40:00 • 5K Checkpoint: Sub-21:00

💪 Keys to Success

  1. Easy runs are sacred. Keep long runs at 6:00/km or slower. Use strides/fast finishes to scratch the speed itch, but don't race them.
  2. Adjust for conditions. On hot days or poor sleep, slow by 5-10 sec/km. It's better to complete at 4:10 than quit at 4:00.
  3. Stitch prevention. Practice race nutrition on long runs (weeks 12 & 13). No solid food 2 hours before hard sessions.
  4. Strength matters. 20 mins of core/glute work 2x/week (Mon/Fri). Strong glutes = stable hips = no form breakdown.
